About AllenConnect

AllenConnect is a private social platform exclusively for the Allenhouse community. Whether you're a current student, alumnus, or professor, AllenConnect helps you:

  • Connect with fellow alumni and classmates
  • Stay updated on college events and announcements
  • Share achievements and opportunities
  • Build a professional network rooted in trust and shared experience
